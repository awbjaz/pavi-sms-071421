Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise (On-premise and Odoo.SH) as well as Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.

I need an extra feature in this app. How can I request it?

You can contact us to request a customization in the app.

Does the Salesforce Odoo Connector allow two-way synchronization of Products?

Yes, you can export Products from Odoo to Salesforce as well as import Products from Salesforce to Odoo. If the products are already present on Odoo as well as Salesforce, you can establish a synchronization between them using the connector. You can also activate synchronization of upcoming product in Odoo by checking the checkbox in settings, it will automatically create product in Salesforce too.
